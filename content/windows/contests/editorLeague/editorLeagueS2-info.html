Rules/FAQ
Click To Reveal Rules/FAQ
  • Your team must consist of exactly 3 members.
  • You must be in the ult & co. & visionary studios Discord server.
  • Each round may limit teams to a specific aesthetic or genre.
  • Teams are required to use one song per round that is not globally region blocked on YouTube (to check for this, reupload the song and look in YouTube Studio)
  • the final edit must be a single, cohesive video (MEP). Multiple songs can be used if they thematically match and contribute to a creative whole.
  • Only the provided clips must be used in your edit, though they may be remade in 3D, provided they stay true to the original content.
  • Minimum edit length is 20 seconds, excluding any intros or black screens.
  • Your final render must include the logos provided in some way.
  • Rules may change periodically.
  • AI should only be used as a tool and not as the final product.
