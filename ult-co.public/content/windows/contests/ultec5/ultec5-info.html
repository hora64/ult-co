Rules/FAQ
Click To Reveal Rules/FAQ
  • All songs are allowed (don't use a song that will get globally region blocked on YouTube, and to check for this, reupload the song and look in YouTube Studio).
  • Generative AI can only be used as a tool and not as the final product.
  • You can use the clips provided for the #ultec5 bracket you're competing in, create/recreate clips in 3D software that should resemble an in-game clip (like the memeycornys #ultec3 viridian edit), or record your own footage using the game's replay mode; additional clip requests are welcome.
  • Cinematics, 3D scenes/objects, and any other effects are allowed.
  • Collaborations are allowed.
  • You must use the logos provided by the sponsors and ult & co.
  • The minimum video length for submissions is 1 minute for Fortnite, 45 seconds for Valorant and COD, with hybrid edits requiring 1 minute 15 seconds for Fortnite & Valorant/COD, 1 minute for COD & Valorant, and 1 minute 45 seconds for a combination of Fortnite, Valorant, and COD, with hybrid edits judged more harshly.
  • Post your submission on YouTube and/or Streamable with the #ultec5 hashtag (tag us).
  • Blank/static footage and outros don't count towards the submission length.
